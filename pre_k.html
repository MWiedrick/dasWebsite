Pre-Kindergarten

DAS gives you a variety of schedule options for Pre-Kindergarten. You can choose from the AM session (8:45-11:40am) or PM session (12:00 noon – 2:55pm) or Full Day (8:45am – 2:55pm). All programs are available 3-5 days a week and are for children 3 to 5 years of age. All children must be three years old by September 1st of the year they are starting and completely potty trained to attend.

Your child will learn language and literacy skills, science, mathematics, social studies, physical education, music and art. Teachers plan weekly themes with fun activities and learning opportunities. Each week also includes time in the gym and the library, in addition to yoga and music with special teachers.

Parents can take advantage of our AM and PM Care sessions as well, to accommodate almost any schedule.

We are currently full for the 2017/2018 Pre-K school year due to our limited classroom size. If you would like to be added to our wait list please turn in your registration form to Room 5 at Duniway Elementary School or email your registration form to director@daschildcare.org. We do not need any type of payment at this time.
