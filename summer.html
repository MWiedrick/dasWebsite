Summer Camp

Our summer camp is for children 4-12 years of age and is open to school families and the surrounding community. Regular camp hours run from 8:00am to 4:00pm, Monday through Friday. We also offer AM and PM Care sessions during summer which extend our hours of operation to 7:00am to 6:00pm. It is our goal to meet all familiesâ€™ scheduling needs.

Each summer camp week focuses on a theme and includes at least one enrichment visitor or field trip. DAS Summer Camp will engage your child in art, physical activity, music and creative play!

To register for DAS Summer Camp, please complete the registration form included in the brochure. We need one form for each child. Return the forms to us with your registration fee and deposit(s). Please let us know right away if your plans change; we will be holding a space for your child for all of the weeks you select. We will also need a signed permission form if you would like us to apply sunblock to your child.

We are looking forward to another fun summer!
